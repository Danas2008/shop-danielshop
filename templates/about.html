{% trans "About DanielsPrints" %}

{% blocktrans %}DanielsPrints creates personalized 3D-printed keepsakes that turn your favourite moments — anniversaries, first dates, a special song, a meaningful place — into a tangible memento. Every piece is printed on demand and fully customized by you.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}It started with a simple idea: receipts and tickets from a first date or a wedding day are often the only physical trace of a memory that means everything. We wanted to give those moments a home — something you can actually hold, put on a shelf, or give as a gift, instead of a photo buried in a camera roll.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "How we work" %}

{% blocktrans %}We're a small, independent print shop based in the Czech Republic, focused on quality and quick turnaround. Every plaque is designed and prepared by hand after you submit your order — no mass production, no templates shared between customers. You customize it in our live builder, we review it, print it on our own 3D printers, and pack it carefully for shipping.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "What we stand for" %}

{% blocktrans %}Quality materials, honest pricing, and a fast reply if something needs fixing. We personally check every order before it goes to print, and we're always happy to make small adjustments if you spot a typo or change your mind about a detail.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Have an idea that doesn't quite fit our current product types? Reach out — we're always looking to add new ways to print your memories.{% endblocktrans %}
